Owner: "Yes, this is the owner."
You: "My name is {Your Name} with Swift Mover Acquisition Group. How are you today?"
(Pause for response)
You: "I am calling because we help local moving companies get more jobs. Are you available to take on more jobs right now, or is your schedule completely full?"

Route A: Account admits "Yes, we definitely need more jobs / we have openings"
"That is exactly why we reached out. What lanes or states are you looking to add more volume to right now?"
Route B: Account drops standard friction ("We already have marketing", "Slow season", etc.)
"Completely understand. Most owners we talk to have systems running. We don't want to break your current pipeline—we operate strictly as an overflow system catching the premium long-distance runs or weekend clicks your staff slips onto voicemail. If we brought you 2 verified cross-country bookings next week on a pure performance baseline, could your trucks execute?"
